Our Vision

Vismathe Natural Farm is committed to providing pure, authentic, and chemical-free farm produce that preserves the goodness of nature and supports healthier living.

Meet Our Founder

The visionary behind Vismathe Farm Produce. With academic excellence and decades of experience from ICAI, IIPM, and CUK, he is passionate about natural, sustainable farming.

Our Farming Philosophy

At Vismathe, we carefully nurture every coconut and Pooja banana without chemicals, pesticides, or artificial treatments. By using only farm-based fertilizers, we ensure every product is fresh, healthy, and natural.

Our Star Product: Pooja (Elakki) Banana

Vismathe’s Pooja bananas, also known as Elakki, are nurtured with care using chemical-free and sustainable farming practices. These sweet, aromatic bananas are perfect for religious offerings as well as everyday consumption, providing nature’s authentic taste without harmful pesticides or additives.

Why Choose Our Pooja Bananas?

  • Naturally sweet and nutritious: Packed with essential vitamins, minerals, and dietary fiber.
  • Eco-friendly cultivation: Grown sustainably with proper use of fertilizers to enrich the soil and promote healthy growth.
  • Perfect for Pooja and everyday use: Ideal for religious rituals or as a wholesome, healthy snack.
  • Pure and safe: Free from chemicals and artificial treatments, ensuring quality you can trust.

Cultivation & Care

Our bananas are carefully fertilized and nurtured to maximize flavor, aroma, and nutrition. Each plant is tended with respect for the environment, ensuring that the fruit you enjoy is both healthy for you and kind to the planet.
